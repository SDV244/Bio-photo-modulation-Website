What is Photobiomodulation?

Photobiomodulation (PBM), also known as Low-Level Laser Therapy (LLLT), is the use of specific wavelengths of light (red and near-infrared) to stimulate healing and reduce inflammation.

It works at a cellular level, enhancing the function of mitochondriaâ€”the powerhouses of your cells.

How It Works

1. Absorption

Red (660nm) and Near-Infrared (850nm) light is absorbed by the skin and deeper tissues.

2. Stimulation

The light stimulates Cytochrome C Oxidase in the mitochondria, boosting ATP production.

3. Regeneration

Increased cellular energy leads to faster repair, reduced oxidative stress, and lower inflammation.
