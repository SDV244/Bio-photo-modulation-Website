The San Francisco 49ers Edge

The San Francisco 49ers were the first professional sports franchise to install a dedicated "Recovery Room" featuring full-body light therapy devices directly in their stadium.

Elliot Williams, Director of Functional Performance, cites photobiomodulation as a critical tool for managing the physical demands of an NFL season.

"We use light therapy to support the body's natural inflammation process, increase cellular regeneration, and optimize sleep. It's a game changer for recovery."

[Image: 49ers Recovery Room]

Key Applications in the NFL

1. Inflammation Control

Post-game inflammation is a major hurdle. PBM helps modulate the inflammatory response, allowing players to return to training sooner.

2. Travel & Sleep

With cross-country travel, circadian rhythms are disrupted. The 49ers use light therapy to regulate sleep cycles and combat jet lag.

3. Cellular Regeneration

Red light stimulates collagen production and cellular repair, essential for healing micro-tears in muscles after high-impact plays.
